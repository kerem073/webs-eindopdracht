Who we are

We are Spacerra. We are a big private company and we do research about Earth, Mars and Jupiter. We have been in business for quite a while now. Think about almost 18 years! That is indeed a long time. We lead teams of the most experienced and smartest researchers, so we now what we are doing and that our information is reliable. Our headquarter is based in Amsterdam, because in the Netherlands are many talented people and those people could join the company and make our company even stronger. What makes us so special is the way we work. We don't have scheduled times when we work. It gives us a lot of freedom. Our mission is to find the perfect planet were the human race can live. we'll make sure that the human race can live on another planet when planet Earth becomes uninhabitable.
